HITDKE's Contribution to TinyOS

This page summarizes all projects contributed by Data and Knowledge Engineering (DKE) Lab at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), P. R. China.

Please send general questions or requirements to SHI, Shengfei (coordinator of the Sensor Networks Group at HIT DKE Lab). For technical questions, please contact the maintainers of respective projects as listed below.

1. TOSSIM DB

TOSSIM DB is the Data-Enriched Simulator for TinyOS applications (formerly known as the "SyntheticSensor for TOSSIM").

When a TinyOS application is compiled with the TOSSIM DB extension, its Sensor components are transparently bound to a customized data source (i.e. can be real historical data file or synthetic data generator), and they yield the data *as if* sensed by the application in real time. In this manner, TOSSIM DB offers more realistic and more thorough simulation scenarios than the official DemoSensorC solution of TinyOS 2.x (see TEP109).

TOSSIM DB is developed and maintained by LIU Yu. Please visit http://openjudge.net/~liuyu/Project/TossimDB to learn more.
