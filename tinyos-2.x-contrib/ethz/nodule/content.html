Introduction

High-Performance/Low-Power prototype with Atmels 32-bit MCU (AT32UC3B0256) and Chipcons RF radio (CC2500).

The AT32UC3B MCU features 256K bytes Flash, 32 KBytes SRAM, full-speed (12 Mbps) USB 2.0 with On-The-Go (OTG) capability, delivers 75 DMIPS at 60 MHz, and consumes 23 mA at 3.3V.
