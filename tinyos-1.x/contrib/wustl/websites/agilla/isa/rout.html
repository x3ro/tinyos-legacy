0 = operation failed (agent did not get an ACK; tuple may or may not have been inserted)

1 = operation succeeded

Description

Inserts a tuple into a remote hosts's tuple space. The tuple is accessible to anybody. The first variable in the operand stack must be the address of the destination node. The address may be an (x,y) location or simply a value equal to the destination's TinyOS address.
