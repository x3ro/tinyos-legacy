AcousticRanging:

Author/Contact: miklos.maroti@vanderbilt.edu (Miklos Maroti, ISIS, Vanderbilt)

DESCRIPTION:

The AcousticRangingActuator and AcousticRangingSensor interfaces are used to initiate
and receive acoustic ranging measurements. Currently, these interfaces are implemented
by the OutsideRangingActuator and OutsideRangingSensor components.
