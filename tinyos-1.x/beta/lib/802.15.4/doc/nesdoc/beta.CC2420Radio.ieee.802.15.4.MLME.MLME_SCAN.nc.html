Interface: MLME_SCAN

The MLME-SAP scan primitives define how a device can determine the energy usage or the presence or absence of PANs in a communications channel.
Author: Joe Polastre

  • void request (uint8_t ScanType, uint32_t ScanChannels, uint8_t ScanDuration) Used to initiate a channel scan over a given list of channels.

Events

  • void confirm(IEEE_status status, uint8_t ScanType, uint32_t UnscannedChannels, uint8_t ResultListSize, uint8_t *EnergyDetectList, PANDescriptor_t *PANDescriptorList) Reports the results of the channel scan request

    Parameters:

    status - The status of the scan request

    ScanType - The type of scan performed

    UnscannedChannels - The 27 LSBs indicate which channels are not scanned (1 = not scanned)

    ResultListSize - The number of elements reterned in the appropriate results list

    EnergyDetectList - The list of energy measurements, one for each channel searched during an ED scan

    PANDescriptorList - The list of PAN descriptors, one for each beacon found during an active or passive scan

Commands - Details

request

void request(uint8_t ScanType, uint32_t ScanChannels, uint8_t ScanDuration)

Used to initiate a channel scan over a given list of channels. A device can use a channel scan to measure the energy on the channel, search for the coordinator with which it is associated, or search for all coordinators transmitting beacon frames within the POS of the scanning device.

Parameters:

ScanType - The type of scan performed

ScanChannels - The 27 LSBs indicate which channels are to be scanned (1 = scan)

ScanDuration - Value used to calculate the length of time to spend scanning each channel for ED, active, and passive scans. This parameter is ignored for orphan scans.
