Interface: MCPS_PURGE

MCPS_PURGE is used to manage MSDU handles in the transaction queue of the MCPS of the MAC sublayer. The MCPS-SAP supports the transport of SSCS protocol data units (SPDUs) between peer SSCS entities.
Author: Joe Polastre

Commands

  • void request (uint8_t msduHandle) Request MSDU to be purged from the transaction queue.

Events

  • void confirm (uint8_t msduHandle, IEEE_status status) Confirm reports the results of a request to purge a data MSDU.
