Interface: MLME_GTS

The MLME-SAP GTS management primitives define how GTSs are requested and maintained. Devcies wishing to use these primitives and GTSs in general will already be tracking the beacons of their PAN coordinators. These GTS management primitives are optional for an RFD.
Author: Joe Polastre

Commands

  • void request (uint8_t GTSCharacteristics, bool SecurityEnable) Request allocation of a new GTS or deallocation from the PAN coordinator.

Events

  • void confirm(uint8_t GTSCharacteristics, IEEE_status status) Reports the results of a request to allocated a new GTS or deallocate an existing GTS

    Parameters:

    GTSCharacteristics - The characteristics of the GTS request

    status - The status of the GTS request

  • void indication(uint16_t DevAddress, uint8_t GTSCharacteristics, bool SecurityUse, uint8_t ACLEntry) Indicates that a GTS has been allocated or that a previous allocated GTS has been deallocated

    Parameters:

    DevAddress - Short address of the device that has been allocated or deallocated a GTS

    GTSCharacteristics - The characteristics of the GTS request

    SecurityUse - TRUE if security is enabled for this transfer

    ACLEntry - The macSecurityMode parameter value from the ACL entry associated with the sender of the data frame
